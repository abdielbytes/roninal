Our Facilities

The School is set in a secured, conducive and easily accessible area within the upscale Uyo Metropolis. The School has play equipment for all ages where children can play and also exercise their body and have Physical Education activities as well. Each classroom has modern furniture and sitting arrangement that is different from the conventional school setting. Each class is carefully designed to suit the targeted age to make comfortable for every child. They are colourful painted to stimulate children‟s learning and display that aids their retention and absorption. Our ICT suite is fully connected to the internet so our children can have access to useful and educative information and to aid their research work. Every child gets a hand-on experience each time they visit the suite. Each child has access to a computer for study. The school has a sound-proof standby generator plant so we have uninterrupted supply of electricity at all times. The school has school shuttle buses with highly responsible, dedicated and experience drivers that run our transport services for our parents who may be in need of these services (This is on request)

HEALTH CENTRE

The School has a health centre where children are given quick treatment or first aid in case of emergencies. However, parents will be informed in such instances and when it is beyond the school, the parent of the child will be contacted promptly and the necessary action taken

FIELD TRIPS

Our School believes that Field trip is an aspect of learning and should form the integral part of education. So, we carefully select and monitor all field trips to ensure that the places visited are of utmost benefit and of relevance to the child‟s exposure. Excursions are taken to places of historical, cultural and social relevance

Roninal International Schools

We are also registered with the Ministry of Education, State Commission on Education, IPC and other educational bodies. We also give opportunities to our staff to attend trainings that are highly beneficial to the teaching practices and our children‟s growth.
